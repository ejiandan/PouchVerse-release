# PouchVerse for Apple TV — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-07

PouchVerse for Apple TV ("the App") is a media‑browsing client. It discovers
PouchVerse hosts (iPhone / iPad / Android devices running PouchVerse) on your
local network and lets you browse and play the photos, music and video those
devices choose to share. This policy explains what the App does and does not do
with your information.

## 1. What we collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or to any third
party.** The App has no analytics, no advertising, and no remote servers
operated by us.

## 2. Information processed on your device

To provide its features, the App processes the following **locally on your Apple
TV and only on your local network**:

- **Local network discovery** — the App uses Bonjour / peer‑to‑peer networking
  to find PouchVerse hosts on the same Wi‑Fi network or via Apple's direct
  (AWDL) links. Only the device name and platform advertised by each host are
  read.
- **Media metadata and thumbnails** — when you connect to a host, the App
  downloads the list of shared media (file names, sizes, types) and their
  thumbnails so it can display your library.
- **Media content** — when you open an item, the App streams that file from the
  host for playback.

## 3. Caching

To make reconnecting fast, the App stores the most recent catalogue and
thumbnails for devices you have connected to in the Apple TV's local cache.
This data never leaves the device and is removed by the system when storage is
reclaimed, or when you delete the App.

## 4. Permissions

- **Local Network** — required to discover and connect to PouchVerse hosts.
  The App makes no connections to the public internet.

## 5. Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.
